About Impact Education Systems

Founded by Amy and Brian Sikora, Impact Education Systems blends visionary leadership, equity-minded practice, and hands-on operational experience in higher education and K-12 systems.

We understand the realities of limited resources, complex regulations, and rising expectations. Our work is grounded in listening, collaboration, and designing solutions your teams can actually use.

  • Community colleges
  • K-12 districts & TAG programs

Support for Institutions

Institutions partner with Impact Education Systems when they need a calm, experienced team to navigate complexity—without losing sight of students.

  • End-to-end reviews of admissions, records, and advising workflows
  • Policy alignment with accreditation, compliance, and equity goals
  • Implementation support for SIS, CRM, and document management tools
  • Facilitated strategy sessions with cross-functional stakeholders

You bring your vision. We bring structure, facilitation, and operational depth so your teams can move from ideas to measurable outcomes.

Support for Families & Students

If you’re feeling lost in testing results, services, or school language, we provide calm, practical guidance. Our individualized support helps you understand your child’s needs, options, and rights so you can advocate with confidence.

  • Review of school records, assessments, and progress data
  • Readable written summaries and recommendation reports
  • Preparation and strategy for IEP, 504, TAG, and placement meetings
  • Coaching on communication with schools and next-step planning
  • College and career readiness planning for complex pathways

About Impact Education Systems

Founded by Amy and Brian Sikora, Impact Education Systems blends visionary leadership, equity-minded practice, and hands-on operational experience in higher education and K-12 systems.

We understand the realities of limited resources, complex regulations, and rising expectations. Our work is grounded in listening, collaboration, and designing solutions your teams and families can actually use.

Support for Institutions

School and district consulting includes SPED, MTSS, 504, and TAG reviews; program improvement; staff training; and strengthening communication with families.

Higher education consulting includes equity and student success strategy, improving student services, online learning program development, inclusive curriculum design, and leadership training.

Technology and systems consulting includes workflow mapping, process optimization, automation, cloud-based tools, registrar system redesign, and adopting modern assessment and reporting solutions.

Support for Families & Students

If you’re feeling lost in testing results, services, or school language, we provide clear, practical guidance. Our support helps you understand your child’s needs, options, and rights so you can advocate with confidence.

Special Education Support & Advocacy: IEP review and explanation. Meeting preparation for IEP, 504, MTSS, and eligibility. Support with eligibility decisions. Behavior, SEL, and executive function guidance. Transition planning between elementary, middle, high school, and adulthood. This is a core part of our work and one of the first services families can access.

Independent Special Education Evaluations: Academic achievement evaluations. Cognitive evaluations. Behavioral and executive function evaluations. Written summaries in parent-friendly language. Help interpreting school evaluations. Guidance for eligibility conversations.

Gifted/TAG & Advanced Learning Support: Support for gifted and twice-exceptional learners. Guidance on enrichment, acceleration, and advanced coursework. Understanding district TAG processes. Practical classroom support recommendations.

Family Consulting & Guidance: One-on-one guidance for families. Advocacy support for school communication and meetings. Help understanding evaluations and school decisions. Support resolving communication breakdowns. Clear, short action plans for next steps.
